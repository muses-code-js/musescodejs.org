What it's all about:

We believe that any woman should try programming at least once in their life. There for we created a community where in friendly atmosphere with great vibe you can try programming first time or if you already code - learn something new about JavaScript and/or Node.js.

Our half day coding bootcamps is also a great opportunity to grow your network and meet new people with similar interests. Come and join our FREE event for women with lunch provided.

What to bring with you:
  • - laptop (if you do not have laptop write us directly - we will figure something out for you)
  • - charger
  • - desire to learn
  • - good mood
Agenda of the day:
  • 9:45 - 10:00 Registration
  • 10:00 - 10:30 Intro + Group formation
  • 10:00 - 11:00 Coffee station
  • 10:30 - 12:00 Coding workshop with help of mentors
  • 12:00 - 12:45 Lunch
  • 12:45 - 2:40 Coding workshop with help of mentors
  • 2:40 - 3:00 Tea with sweets
  • 3:00 - 3:10 Wrap up, next steps

On the day attendees will be divided on 2-3 groups by their skills (complete beginners, intermediate and advanced) with following up short presentations on how to start and following up coding exercises. The format of the event will be code-alone with the explanations in the tasks and help of our mentors.

How much does it cost:

This event is FREE of charge. With healthy lunch and soft drinks provided. It is all possible because of our generous sponsors who covered not only food/drinks but also some important things as venue/advertising and many other expenses!
